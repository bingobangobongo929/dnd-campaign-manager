## SUMMARY: Key Claim Visibility Points

The player knows they can claim a character through:

1. **Homepage notification** (if they have a claimable character)
2. **Campaign dashboard banner** (prominent, at top)
3. **Character view within campaign** (banner at top)
4. **Notification center** (bell icon, dropdown)

All use consistent messaging:
- "Add to Vault" / "Claim to Vault"
- Clear explanation of what they get
- Checkbox options for In-Play and Session 0 copies
