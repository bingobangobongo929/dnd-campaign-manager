1. They are a member of the campaign
2. The DM has explicitly assigned that character to them
3. They haven't already claimed it

### 6.3 Publish/Unpublish Behavior

**When Published:**
- Appears in Discover for all users
- Others can save to their Collection
- Stats tracked (saves, ratings, reviews)

**When Unpublished:**
- Removed from Discover (no new saves)
- **Existing saves are NOT removed** - people keep their copies
- Your template becomes private again
- Can re-publish anytime
